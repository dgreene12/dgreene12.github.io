Service & Engagement

My commitment to service spans community engagement, institutional contributions, and academic pursuits. Through these diverse roles, I strive to make a positive impact and foster meaningful change.

Community Service

Coach for 5-6-year-old basketball in the Durham Parks and Recreation league through Coach2Inspire, mentoring young athletes and promoting teamwork.

Institutional Service

Serving on key Duke University committees, including the Board of Trustees Racial Equity Advisory Council and Undergraduate Education Committee.

Academic Service

Leading research and data analysis for the Durham University-Assisted Community Schools Research Collective, presenting findings to diverse stakeholders.

These service experiences have not only allowed me to contribute to my community and institution but have also enriched my personal and professional growth. I'm committed to continuing this journey of service and positive impact.
